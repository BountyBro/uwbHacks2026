What We're Here For

Contact us if you have any of the following:

  • Questions: Don't understand a concept from our modules? We're happy to help clarify.
  • Feedback: Let us know what you liked, what could be improved, and what topics you'd like us to cover.
  • Suggestions: Have ideas for new modules, features, or improvements? We'd love to hear them.
  • Bug Reports: Found something that doesn't work? Please let us know so we can fix it.
  • Collaboration: Interested in partnering with us or using our materials in your organization?
  • Speaking Opportunities: Interested in having our team speak at your event or organization?
  • Media Inquiries: Press, podcast, or interview requests.

FAQs

Is the content free?

Yes! All of our learning materials are completely free and available to everyone. We believe AI literacy is essential knowledge that should be accessible to all.

Can I use your materials in my classroom?

Absolutely! Our materials are designed to be used in educational settings. We encourage teachers and educators to use them in their classrooms. Please provide attribution to the AI Literacy Learning Lab.

How often are the materials updated?

We regularly update our content to reflect new developments in AI and improve based on user feedback. Major updates are announced through our newsletter and social media.

Do you offer certificates or credentials?

Currently, we don't offer formal certificates. However, we're exploring options for certificates of completion and credentials in the future.

How can I stay updated about new modules?

Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to be notified when new modules and content become available.

Can I contribute to the project?

Yes, we welcome contributions from everyone! Visit our Contribute page to learn how you can help build better AI literacy education.

Privacy & Safety

We take your privacy seriously. We do not collect personal information beyond what's necessary for communication. For details, please review our Privacy Policy on our main website.
