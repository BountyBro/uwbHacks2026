Why AI Literacy Matters

Artificial intelligence is no longer a distant technology. It's integrated into the tools we use daily, the decisions that affect us, and the future we're building. Understanding AI is essential for making informed decisions in an increasingly digital world. This learning lab is designed to equip you with foundational knowledge about artificial intelligence. Whether you're a student, professional, or curious learner, these modules will help you understand how AI works, where it's applied, and how to think critically about AI-generated content. Start with any module that interests you most, or progress through them sequentially for a comprehensive introduction to AI literacy.
